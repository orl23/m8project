Welcome to the Love and War Update

  • Smite released their new Goddess Ishtar. She is a Hybrid hunter that can fight up close and far away, her only weakness is mid range combat with very short Crowd Control and she has an extra mecchanic to release 3 shots within seconds.
  • Ishtar's passive is When she is below 75% health, she can mark the enemy god that damaged her for 10s. Each time she deal damage she gains an attack speed buff, when she recieves a kill or assit she gains a movement speed buff. This passsive has a 20 seconds cooldown.
  • Her first ability, She enhances her basic attacks with three varieties include a basic Strike shot, an explosive arrow(All range), Shotgun arrows(close range), and Sniping arrow(Long range). Fortuntly, she cant hit towers with these abilities.
  • Ishtar's Second ability Rolling Thunder is a Lighting strike in a forward line, damaging the enemies within the area. If the Enemy is hit by it more than once they are mesmerized for 0.75 seconds.
  • Ishtar's third ability is called Jolt, Ishtar charges forward and striking all enemies she meets and if she hits an enemy god she roots them for 1s and backflips off of them. When shes in the air from the backflip, she fires her bow dealing damage around the effected god. This can Crit.
  • Ishtars's Ultimate makes her CC-Immune and summons and charges 5 swords towards the enemy, if the enemy is hit by the sword and if she summons the swords to hit you again you will be stunned for 1s and it deals reduced damage to the enemy god.
